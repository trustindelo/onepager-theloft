Reservations

Living well starts at the table. We believe food is the main ingredient for making life better. Using the freshest local ingredients possible, we prepare food that looks good and tastes even better.

It's our mission to make you feel at home. We value inclusiveness and provide everyone with a space to eat and drink. Whether you're vegan, vegetarian, carnivore, or gluten/lactose intolerant, come and enjoy our mouthwatering meals, prepared the way you like it!

For parties of six or more, we recommend making reservations at least two weeks in advance. For walk-ins, we only seat parties on a first come, first served basis.
